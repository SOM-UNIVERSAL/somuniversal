THE CRAFT PHILOSOPHY

Nature Doesn’t Repeat, Neither Do We

At SOM Universal, we treat every piece of mature pine as a living chronicle. Our material exploration is not about forcing a form upon nature, but about listening to the "fingerprint" of the grain. Each knot, each variance in density, tells a story of decades spent in silence.

We have pioneered a unique process where the natural resins and textures of Indian Pinewood are preserved through meticulous hand-craftsmanship. This results in sculptural objects that breathe, catching the light in ways that change throughout the day—a phenomenon we call the "Heritage Glow."
