COLLECTIBLE OBJECTS

Heritage Glow

An intersection of ancient pine narratives and modern luminescence. Handcrafted to evoke the warmth of a forest's first light.

01 / THE MATERIAL

Mature Pine & Memory

Every piece begins in the high-altitude forests of the Himalayas. We source mature pine that has weathered decades of extreme seasons, resulting in a wood grain that carries a dense, rhythmic history.

The resins are carefully pigmented to match the specific 'glow' of sunset through needle-thin leaves, creating a visual warmth that is felt as much as it is seen.

Origin Profile

"The fingerprint of nature is never repeated."

02 / THE CRAFT

Precision & Hand-Finishing

The Cut

Utilizing state-of-the-art CNC technology for architectural precision, ensuring structural integrity while respecting the wood's organic fiber.

The Polish

Twelve stages of hand-sanding with progressively finer grits to achieve a tactile surface that feels like silk to the touch.

The Infusion

Signature Heritage resin is vacuum-poured into the natural voids of the wood, permanently locking the forest's narrative in light.

03 / THE EXPERIENCE

A Living Presence

"It doesn't just sit in a room; it transforms the space into a sanctuary."

Heritage Glow reacts to the time of day. In the morning, it catches the natural light; by night, it emits a low, pulse-like warmth that anchors the atmosphere of any modern interior.
